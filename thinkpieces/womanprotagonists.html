woman protagonists

Lately, I've been thinking about the shows that I have been really into. some may say i'm hyperfixated.
- Frieren: Beyond Journey's End
- Arcane
- The Apothecary Diaries
All of these have very compelling lead woman characters. And it feels very comforting to have these characters written so well. Frieren, Jinx, and Maomao all feel like people who are just really cool. They have their passions from magic to tinkering to ... poison drinking (not encouraging this one to people). They have downfalls and triumphs. Quirks and flaws. The best word I can use to describe the feeling of watching these shows is cozy.
In a lot of cases where women are portrayed as "empowered" in film and media, it sometimes feels like directors interpret that as "show proof that a woman can do more or equal to a man", which feels forced and undermines gender equality (as knuckles once said... ).

in reality, a lot of people are better at things than others, independent of gender. and it really sucks to see society use sexism as a way to deny this reality. or woman having to use "being better than a man" as external motivation or to prove themselves. or men who have internalized sexism by their community to feel insecure if a woman beats you. It often feels like a zero-sum game â€” like we have to diminish one group just to uplift another onto level ground. but true equality shouldn't come at the cost of someone else's value. rather creating space to thrive and support others hurt. so i think we should take our talents in vacuum and just say we are valuable for the merit of our character rather than gender. we are teetering on the edge - if not in the midst - of a gender war with social media filled with mutual hatred and confusion to the opposite gender. and too quickly is it turning to women resenting men and men's emotions being dismissed or unheard. so we should all be a little bit better to each other. that's all.

highly recommend the shows above!
